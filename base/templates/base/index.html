3D Court Grid Mapping

Our advanced 3D Court Grid Mapping overlays a detailed grid across the entire tennis court up to the net level, enabling precise spatial analysis of every play. This grid facilitates accurate tracking of ball trajectories, bounce points (p1bb, p2bb), contact points (p1cp, p2cp), and net clearances (p1nc, p2nc), providing a comprehensive view of the game dynamics.

Combined with player keypoint detection and bounding boxes, the grid helps analyze movements and shot placements with unparalleled precision. Whether you're studying a serve's trajectory, a rally's intensity, or a player's court coverage, this visualization empowers coaches and players with actionable insights to elevate performance and strategy.
