About Us

From the Developer's Desk: Our Story

My journey with badminton began long before I ever touched a smartphone or dived into the world of app development. As a passionate player, badminton wasn’t just a sport to me—it was a way of life. The thrill of each rally, the joy of every smash, and even the frustration of missed shots shaped who I am today.

Like many athletes, I faced my fair share of challenges. There were times when progress felt stagnant, moments when understanding my weaknesses seemed impossible, and instances where I wished for a clearer way to track my improvement.

That's when the idea hit me: What if there was a way to visualize my growth, to get real, actionable insights that could truly elevate my game? Combining my love for badminton with my technical background , Shuttle Smart Vision was born.
